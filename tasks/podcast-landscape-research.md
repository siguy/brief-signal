---

## Key for YouTube Extraction

YouTube availability is critical because your existing pipeline already extracts from YouTube playlists. Podcasts with full episodes on YouTube can be fed directly into your extraction workflow.

- **YouTube: YES (full episodes)** = full video episodes posted, extractable
- **YouTube: YES (clips only)** = only clips/highlights, limited extraction value
- **YouTube: NO** = audio-only distribution, would need RSS/transcript extraction
